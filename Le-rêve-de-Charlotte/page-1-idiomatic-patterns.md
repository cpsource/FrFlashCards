Here are the French expressions from the text:

• **Allez !** - Come on! / Go! (encouragement)
• **Allez Charlotte, allez !** - Come on Charlotte, go! / Let's go Charlotte!
• **Allez ! Cours !** - Come on! Run!
• **Le ballon, Charlotte ! Passe !** - The ball, Charlotte! Pass!
• **BUT !** - GOAL!
• **Oui !!! Bravo !!!** - Yes!!! Bravo!!!
• **Tout le monde crie** - Everyone shouts/screams
• **le coup de sifflet** - the whistle blow
• **la fin de la partie** - the end of the game/match
• **C'est la victoire pour l'équipe** - It's victory for the team
• **en bonne position pour** - in a good position to
• **La tension est forte entre** - The tension is strong between

These are like idiomatic patterns in code - they work as complete units and are commonly used together in their specific contexts (in this case, sports commentary and match descriptions).
